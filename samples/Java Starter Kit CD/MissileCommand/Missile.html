Missile Commando

Press the spacebar to pause/continue the game.

Be sure to try Rocks In Space!, written by Andrew Scherpbier .

The object of the game is to destroy the incoming missiles before they hit one of the 5 bases. Point and click to destroy missiles. New bases are created every 5000 points. Each level increases the speed and amount of incoming missiles. The game is over when all the bases are destroyed. Watch out for Magenta missiles! Click in the missile commando window to start a new game. Good Luck!

Here's the Java source.

Bugs:

  • This applet has been known to crash Netscape, AppletViewer, and X servers.
  • New bases are always created from left to right.
  • The sound effects may sound choppy.
