After minimization

The idea of logic minimization is to find a cheap realization for a given logic function. With KV diagrams, one searches and marks loops in the diagram. Each loop corresponds to a new term that covers some minterms of the function. Note how the resulting PLA is much cheaper than the initial minterm realization!
