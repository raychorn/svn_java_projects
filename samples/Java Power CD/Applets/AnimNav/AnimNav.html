AnimNav.class

This applet loads a series of URLs, their descriptions and a series of images, usually an animation. A translation table links the given URLs to frames(or sets of frames) of the animation. Clicking on the applet while a given image is showing navigates to the URL linked to that image. Optionally, when the mouse is not over the applet an timer will auto advance through the images. It uses off-screen buffering to avoid flicker. The full documentation for the APPLET tag is in the source code, AnimNav.java.
